Institutional profile

Kiambu-Karai Empowerment Forum

KKEF is the digital spine connecting community-based organisations, SACCOs, environmental stewards, and county technical teams. The platform unifies projects, proposals, verified giving, GIS intelligence, and live engagement under one policy-aligned operating model.

Governance stack

Super administrators, forum administrators, group leaders, stakeholders, donors, and members each receive curated workspaces with CSRF-hardened APIs, audit trails, and optional TOTP devices.

Deployment posture

Docker, Nginx, Gunicorn/Daphne, PostgreSQL, and Redis channels match county-scale resilience requirements while keeping edge accessibility for community volunteers.
