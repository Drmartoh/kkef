About KKEF

An umbrella for every community enterprise.

We choreograph county technical teams, philanthropic partners, and hyper-local leaders into a single empowerment stack: projects, proposals, verified giving, and field intelligence.

Vision

A Kiambu-Karai citizenry fully equipped to co-steward social, ecological, and economic resilience.

Mission

Blend data, culture, and capital to unlock transparent growth for every registered group.

  • Integrity & fiscal discipline
  • Co-creation & inclusion
  • Climate-smart livelihoods
  • Evidence-led decisions

Leadership philosophy

Rotating stewardship with audited decision trails.

Forum administrators curate approvals, group leaders steward field truth, donors track capital outcomes, while stakeholders digitally co-sign major escalations.
